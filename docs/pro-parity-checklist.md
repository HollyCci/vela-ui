## Completion Contract

- `Browser smoke` means the local showcase page renders real Vela components and exercises at least one meaningful browser interaction for the component.
- `Manual visual`, `Manual DOM/CSS`, and `Manual interaction` stay `pending` until a same-viewport comparison against the live HeroUI Pro page has been performed and recorded.
- This checklist is a progress artifact, not completion proof. The goal remains open while any row has pending manual parity work or an open gap.
